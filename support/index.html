Support

Questions, bugs, or ideas for Chaotic Clock? We'd love to hear from you.

Contact

Email chaotic-clock@proton.me and include your device model and iOS version if you're reporting a problem — it helps a lot.

Common questions

  • My daily challenge attempt was wasted. The daily run starts the moment you tap the card and allows one attempt per day (UTC). A new challenge unlocks at midnight UTC.
  • My scores aren't on the leaderboard. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in iOS Settings. Scores submitted while signed out are queued and sent after you sign in.
  • How is the safe zone judged? A stop counts when the hand is inside the green zone around 12 o'clock — the closer to dead-center, the better (PERFECT beats GOOD).
